Style the background. Even if you choose not to render the background, its bounding-box dimensions will be used for clipping. Note that the background dimensions and color information will always be passed on to the final generated result. It is actually recommended to draw the background using Canvas or WebGL primitives instead of baking it in: this way you can do more precise hit-testing and you can make the background color respond to mouse, touch or other state-changes.
